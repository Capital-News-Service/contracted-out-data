Contracted out: How Maryland school districts do business

The Calvert County Public Schools board made 816 recorded payments above $25,000 from FY2019 to FY2022 to 354 total distinct vendors. The school board paid these major vendors a total of $368,063,927 over that period.

This data does not contain payments to most local, state and federal government agencies such as the Internal Revenue Service or Maryland Board of Education, which are often the top vendor, because of inconsistencies in interpreting the reporting law between counties.
